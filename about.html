Our Story

A neighbourhood clinic with a modern outlook.

Oro Dental Care began with a simple idea: people in Muzaffarpur should not have to choose between advanced dental care and a kind, familiar experience.

Located near Clock Tower on Saraiyaganj Road, our clinic brings preventive, restorative and cosmetic dentistry together in one calm, carefully maintained space. We take time to understand concerns, explain findings in plain language and recommend care that is genuinely useful.
